FAQ

Arkansas Epoxy Flooring Questions

How much does an epoxy garage floor cost in Arkansas?

Most homeowners in Arkansas pay between $1,200 and $7,750 for a professionally installed epoxy garage floor, depending on garage size, current floor condition, and the finish you choose. Use the instant quote tool above for a price range based on your specific garage.

What's the difference between solid, flake, and metallic epoxy?

Solid color is the most budget-friendly and gives a clean, classic look. Flake (or chip) systems add texture and hide dirt and tire marks well, making them popular for daily-use garages. Metallic epoxy creates a premium, three-dimensional marbled look and costs more due to the extra material and installer skill involved.

Does floor condition affect the price?

Yes. Cracks, oil stains, or previous coatings that need to be ground off all add prep time and cost. A floor in good condition needs only surface prep and etching, while major damage may require crack repair or diamond grinding before coating.
